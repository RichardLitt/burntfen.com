Taxacom is a mailing list that started some time in the forever ago, and ran until it was suddenly cut off at the end of 2025. It was one of the primary places for discussions of taxanomy and nomenclature as a listserve or public forum. I, together with Shinichi Nakahara and my mentor from the Royal Society Te Apārangi Neil Birrell, restarted it as a Google Group to allow continued discussion. Join us.

The image is from [this Wikimedia file](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Cetonia_aurata_take_off_composition_05172009.jpg) and is in the public domain.
