I was one of the committee members for [PythonNZ](https://python.nz/), a community of Python users in Aotearoa New Zealand, from 2024-2026. We hold meetups and host an annual event called KiwiPycon. I'm still part fo the community. While I don't use Python in everything, I do use it almost every day I am on a computer, and it is great to be able to support and be a member of a local tech community.

I gave a talk at the first KiwiPycon I attended, in 2024, on iNaturalist and eBird. You can watch it [here](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-KkQEzmOOBY&t=1s). I gave another one the next year, on [OpenSustain.tech](https://opensustain.tech/), which you can watch [here](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kr8BP7eBCsI).

I am helping to organize [Kiwi PyCon 2026](https://kiwipycon.nz/), in the west island of Australia! You should come!
