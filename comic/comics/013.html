RIP Robert



4.16.2008

-----

...for all the dross everyone throws at his writing, I really do like Jordan. I've read his books five times: I'm the only person I know who has. He's not so bad. After all: "I remember when he was first named the Betrayer of Hope. After he betrayed the Gates of Heaven and carried the Shadow down into the Rorn M'doi and the heart of Satelle. Hope seemed to die that day. Culan Cuhan wept." I mean, come on. It may need the 7-book build up he gives it, but that is a really cool line. And the Ashaman are the best. Long live Elyas!...
