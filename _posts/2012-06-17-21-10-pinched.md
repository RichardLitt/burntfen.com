On the Metro in Paris,  
I imagine her thoughts  
and her destination.  
Then, I see her  
fingers  
drumming lightly  
on the metal pole;  
And the wide gulf  
between synapses and nerves  
opens before me.  
  
If that hand  
held this hand,  
this ocean would be  
just lightly pinched.
